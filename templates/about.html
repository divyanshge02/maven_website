Our Story

"Maven: Pioneering Healthcare Excellence Since 2013"

Welcome to Maven, your trusted partner in healthcare excellence. Launched in 2013, Maven was founded with a clear vision: to revolutionize the healthcare landscape through professional consulting services. As a leading Hospital & Healthcare Consultant agency, we are dedicated to supporting and enhancing the vital work of healthcare institutions.

At the helm of Maven is Hardyesh Singh Gehlot, MBA, M.Phil (HHSM), our esteemed Managing Director (M: 9460200287). With his extensive expertise and leadership, Maven has steadily grown to become a beacon of innovation and reliability in the healthcare consulting sector.

Since our inception, we have been committed to the world of healthcare rejuvenation. We pride ourselves on offering a comprehensive suite of services designed to address the diverse needs of hospitals and healthcare organizations. Our core offerings include:

  • Doctor and Hospital Staff Recruitment Services: Connecting talented professionals with the institutions that need them most, ensuring optimal staffing and patient care.
  • Business Development & Strategies Planning: Crafting robust strategies to foster growth, efficiency, and sustainability for healthcare businesses.
  • New Hospital Project Management: Guiding the seamless execution of new hospital projects from conception to completion.
  • Healthcare System Development: Designing and implementing advanced healthcare systems that enhance operational flow and patient outcomes.
  • Hospital IT Consultancy: Providing expert advice and solutions for integrated hospital information technology, ensuring cutting-edge digital infrastructure.
  • Hospital Staff Training and Development Services: Empowering healthcare professionals with the knowledge and skills necessary to deliver exceptional care and adapt to evolving industry standards.

At Maven, we believe in a holistic approach to healthcare consulting. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities within the sector, and our dedicated team works collaboratively with clients to deliver tailored, effective, and sustainable solutions.

Join us in our mission to elevate healthcare standards. We are Maven, and we are committed to building a healthier future, together.
